Introduction

In 2001, I earned the opportunity to be hired by a Winston-Salem, North Carolina startup called “PointDx”. At that point in life, I really didn't have enough life experience to be conscious of the special privilege I had been given. I had no comparison; only had mundane, predictable office jobs with low expectations and salary to match. What a delight it turned out to be, despite the long hours, to really be a significant part of something and to see my efforts put to immediate, practical use. I learned much there, and in the other places I've worked, mostly smaller companies of the same type; where things seem to move at a very high velocity in high energy environments.

I have since worked for a wide range of organizations of varying sizes and types, yet what has remained unchanged for me is the value I put on accomplishing meaningful things and solving interesting problems independent of the obstacles that arise. I value working with a team of humble, capable people who are unsatisfied with “hacks”; people with the wisdom to compromise on a “quick fix” when appropriate, but insist on keeping the ship pointed in the right direction.

This is the workplace I desire. The details of my technical experiences are available below, on my resume. A fair cross-section of my work can be found on my GitHub account. I look forward to having further conversations in order to find a match and get to work on accomplishing great things!

Thanks and best wishes.

-Mike
